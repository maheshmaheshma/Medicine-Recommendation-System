About Health Care Center

Welcome to the Health Care Center Medicine Recommendation System! Our goal is to help you understand your symptoms better and provide recommended medications, precautions, and lifestyle tips.

This system uses machine learning and medical datasets to analyze symptoms and predict possible diseases. It's designed to assist users in making more informed decisions about their health.

Please remember this tool is for informational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider for accurate diagnosis and treatment.
